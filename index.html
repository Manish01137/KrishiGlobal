Who We Are

A Legacy in
Agricultural Trade

Krishi Global Industries is a Gujarat-based agricultural commodity enterprise with deep roots in India's farming and trade ecosystem. We specialise in the procurement, storage, and distribution of agricultural goods across domestic and global markets.

Operating from the heart of APMC New Cotton Market in Dholka, we leverage prime godown infrastructure and decades of industry expertise to deliver reliable, quality-assured trade services to all our partners and clients.
