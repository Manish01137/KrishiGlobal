Who We Are

Rooted in the Soil,
Built for the Market

Krishi Global Industries is a trusted exporter of high-quality agricultural products from India. With strong sourcing networks across major farming regions, we ensure that every product meets international standards of quality, purity, and consistency.

We specialise in exporting coriander seeds, dried chillies, and rice to clients across the globe. Our commitment lies in delivering fresh, naturally processed, and carefully packaged goods that preserve authentic Indian flavour.

With a customer-first approach and reliable logistics, we aim to build long-term partnerships worldwide — bridging India's agricultural heritage with global demand.

What We Export

Our Products

Every product we export is sourced directly from trusted Indian farms, cleaned, graded, and packaged to meet international quality standards — ready for bulk and retail export worldwide.

01
Coriander Seeds

Premium-grade coriander seeds sourced from the finest farms in India. Known for their rich aroma and natural flavour, ideal for culinary and industrial use.

  • High purity & uniform size
  • Strong natural aroma
  • Hygienically cleaned & processed
  • Export-quality packaging
02
Dried Red Chilli

Carefully selected and sun-dried red chillies that deliver vibrant colour, strong pungency, and authentic taste. Ideal for global markets.

  • Rich colour & high heat level
  • Low moisture content
  • Available in multiple varieties
  • Cleaned and sorted for export
03
Rice

We offer premium varieties of Indian rice known for their quality, taste, and aroma, suitable for global markets and bulk export.

  • Long grain & rich texture
  • Naturally processed
  • Consistent quality supply
  • Suitable for bulk export

How It Works

Our Export Process

01
Sourcing

Directly from trusted farms. We maintain relationships with verified farmers across major growing regions to secure the best-quality produce at origin.

02
Quality Check

Multi-level inspection at sourcing and processing stages. Every batch is tested for purity, moisture content, and grade before packaging.

03
Processing

Cleaning, grading, and packaging to international standards. Goods are prepared in export-ready condition with proper documentation and labelling.
