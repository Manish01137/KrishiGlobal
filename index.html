Who We Are

Rooted in the Soil,
Built for the Market

Krishi Global Industries is a Gujarat-based agricultural trade and storage enterprise with a singular focus: delivering reliable, transparent, and quality-assured commodity services from the heart of Dholka's APMC New Cotton Market.

We operate Godown No. 8 — a strategically positioned storage facility at one of Gujarat's most active regulated markets. From procurement and warehousing to direct market operations near Ambika Circle, every function is built on precision, compliance, and a deep respect for the agricultural value chain.

Our GST-registered, APMC-compliant business bridges the gap between Gujarat's farmers and the larger domestic and international commodity trade ecosystem — with a commitment to honesty, accountability, and long-term partnerships.

What We Do

Our Core Services

01
Storage & Godown Operations

We offer secure, APMC-registered warehouse infrastructure at Godown No. 8, New Cotton Market. Our facility is engineered for agricultural commodity storage — ensuring proper conditions, safe handling, and reliable access throughout the year. From short-term holding to extended warehousing, we serve traders, farmers, and businesses alike.

02
Commodity Trade & Procurement

From cotton and grains to spices and agri-produce, we facilitate end-to-end commodity procurement and distribution. Our deep-rooted presence within the APMC ecosystem enables transparent, fair pricing — connecting Gujarat's farming communities with reliable domestic and export market channels.

03
APMC Market Operations

As a registered market participant, we actively operate within the APMC framework near Ambika Circle, Dholka. Our market operations include commodity trading, price discovery, buyer-seller facilitation, and logistics coordination — giving all trade partners seamless access to Gujarat's regulated wholesale ecosystem.
